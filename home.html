COLLEGE INFORMATION

PIMPRI CHINCHWAD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (PCCOE) IS A ENGINEERING COLLEGE IN PCMC INDIA ESTABLISHED IN 1984 AS POLYTECHNIQUE GAINT IN PCMC AREAS TURNED IN FOR ENGUINEERING DEGREE IN 1999.

IT IS FIRST COLLEGE CONSIDERED OF FOR DEVELOPERS IN PCNDTA PLANNINGS.PCCOE IS LOOCATED NEAR GANESH TALAW IN PIMPRI CHINCHWAD NEW TOWN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY(PCNDTA).

IT IS ONLY THE SECOND COLLEGE IN PUNE UNIVERSITY TO HAVE STARTED WORKING IN TWO SHIFTS.APART FROM THE REGULAR 1800,MECHANICAL AND ELECTRONICS AND TELE-COMMUNICATION DEPARTMENTS HAVE 60 STUDENTS EACH IN THE SECOND SHIFT.
